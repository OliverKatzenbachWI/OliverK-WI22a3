Do´s / Dont´s in Thailand

    Do´s
  • Be nice -> the locals aren´t just out for money from tourists. A smile can get you far.
  • Go out -> no matter whether you look for relaxion, food, parties, tours or whatever. You can find it.
  • Talk with locals -> everyone selling something advertises it as the best. Talking with people will show you what´s really the best
  • Be respectful -> especially at tempels. Religion still plays an active role in the daily life so they can take some things personal
  • Go to markets -> great food and entertainment awaits you
  • Try a massage -> it costs nothing so even if you end up not liking it
      • Dont´s
  • Talk about Royalty -> they still have a king so there are punishments for insulting royals.
  • Eat western style -> there are a lot of restaurants offering food like burgers, pizza or other western food. You´re in Thailand why would you eat it?
  • Grab or put your feet somewhere you can´t see. There´s scorpions and snakes!
  • Mess around with security/police -> they have a lot of authority there so don´t take them as a joke.
  • Go to temples in very short clothing -> if your clothing is thought to not be appropriate you won´t be allowed to enter. For example some places require you to cover your legs entirely. Not preparing accordingly means you might have to go around with style for a day
