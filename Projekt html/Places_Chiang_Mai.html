Chiang Mai

  • Animals
      • In Captivity
        There are Animal parks with Elephants and others with Tigers. Activities like riding Elephants, feeding them, petting Tigers or just taking photos are available here.
      In Wild
        Going off track means you can find free Elephants, Buffalos, Monkeys, Snakes and just about any animal you´re trying to find. Animal encounters will be rather rare when staying on tourist paths so I recommend getting a local to show you around and go on a hike.
  • Temples
      • Wat Phra That Doi Suthep
        It´s a very well known temple in Chiang Mai, maybe even the most known one. After visiting the Chiang Mai temples, you´ll have seen enough gold for a lifetime.
