Health

    While generally the chance of health issues is low, there are things you should keep in mind
  • Don´t drink water from the water pipe
  • Cheap street food is highly unlikely to be contaminated, but it´s not impossible.
  • If your stomache can´t handle the food it doesn´t mean you have food poisoning.
  • Some of the food can be quite spicy. There´s unlimited awesome non-spicy food so don´t force yourself.
  • There are a lot of stray dogs. While I haven´t seen any commotion just take the anti-rabies shot to be sure.
  • Don´t put your hands or feed in dark spaces where you can´t see. There´s snakes and scorpions in Thailand so you might end up regretting it.
